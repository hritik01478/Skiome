Competitions

Several competitions related to different fields of science and technology, law, management, finance, etc. will be conducted on a regular basis by Skiome. There are intra-schools, intra-towns/cities, inter club and inter towns/cities competitions held where all the schools under Skiome will be taking part.

Participants will be getting a valuable certificate for taking part in the competition. Winners will be getting prizes, goodies and certificates from Skiome.

The winning teams will be given certificates, prizes and goodies. There will be profile upgradation of the winning schools and the students in our application. The motto of conducting such competitions is to create a healthy competitive environment and to give them knowledge about the intellectual levels of the students of the same age groups around them.
