Clubs

Skiome organises a wide variety of clubs which are devoted to increase the skill levels of students in the field of their interest. These clubs conduct various workshops on a regular basis which would introduce students to new technologies and research work going on in their fields. A club would have around 20-25 students. Clubs which are made in schools under Skiome follow a particular hierarchy of professional communication, training and skill transfer.
The hierarchy is as follows:

(I) →Lead (An official working in Skiome who holds the topmost position in that particular field of the club. She/ He acts according to the needs of the clubs and is the person to organise workshops regularly)

(II) →Mentor (Generally a second year college student who will be acting as a mediator for transferring knowledge she/he learns from the lead and teach it to the Core members of the club)

(III) →Core Members (A few students who are the Presidents, Vice-Presidents, etc. of the club whose job will be to gain knowledge and skills from the mentor and then transfer the same to all the members of the club and train them).

Some of the clubs by Skiome are as follows:

→Robotics Club : This club mainly deals with teaching students about sensors, coding chips, designing robotic structures and their assembly , arduino uno etc. This club will help students to assemble, design and organise their own robotic layouts and machines which would help humans ease their lives.

→Science and Innovation Club: The main motto of this club is to discuss and comprehend new ideas, innovations, research works and theories which are developing currently. This club also helps students to discover new and optimal solutions of many scientific problems which in turn boosts their problem solving skills.

→Designing Club: The students in this club are taught how to construct and execute their creativity in fields like industrial design, engineering design, architecture, landscape etc. Students are also taught photo/video editing and designing, posture designing, after-effects, blending etc.

→Coding Club: This club organises various coding and developing competitions and evolves a great coding environment amongst students. The club also deals with teaching about Web and App Development to students and which in turn would help students to assist their own schools in managing and creating their own websites and applications.

→Photography Club: The club would provide resources and will also teach the students about photo/video editing, light rail photography, drone photography etc.

→Finance Club: This club will cover financial education which would include stock market, GDP, GNP, world economic functioning, investment statistics, financial opportunities, worldwide economic issues, historical scams and frauds and the economic developments after them, the loop-holes in the banking systems,the effect of different events of the world on the market prices, money liquidity, assets and liability etc.

→Administration and Management Club: This club mainly deals with educating students on different aspects of the present administration system. The students will be imparted important skills like leadership, decision making, workload management and distribution organisation of systems and businesses effectively and efficiently, influential communication etc.This would help students explore different fields where authority and influential personality plays an important role.
