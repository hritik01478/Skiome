3D Objects

3-D teaching is one of the most effective and interactive ways of teaching in the modern era. This makes concept learning and studying more interesting for the students and they take an interest in the topic. Skiome has a separate team which comprises members who continuously bring in new technologies and better 3-D models for more effective teaching. We also facilitate modifications in these models according to the teachers, which further makes them effective as the teachers can use their innovation in 3-D teaching to the fullest. Every town/city has a centre where the teachers could order the models according to their needs. The delivery service cycle repeats every 4 days.

Some of our models include the brain model, DNA model, globe, mountains, layers of earth, car engine, atomic models, solar system models, concepts of science, etc.

This feature mainly deals with the problem of a less interactive environment in the classes as the students when taught through this method could visualise better than those who are taught through 2-D methods. This builds innovation in the minds of the students which helps them to enhance their creativity in a proper way
