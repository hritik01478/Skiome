Experiments

We conduct a wide variety of experiments ranging from basic to advanced levels. These experiments are conducted in all the schools under Skiome on a regular basis {weekly/monthly}. Some of the initial experiments ranging from basic to advanced levels are:

→Basic : Experiments based on the board curriculum/generally left. Example: Water distillation, circuit designing, rock testing, layer testing, etc.These experiments are designed specifically to assist students in their exams and affirm general concepts.

→Medium: These include equipment which are costly for the schools and are difficult to maintain and repair. Because of such reasons, they are rarely available in schools.

For example: Plank’s constant apparatus, fractional distillation, chromatography, architecture designing, rainwater harvesting, water level testing, etc.

→Advanced: These are equipment required to understand higher level concepts usually theoretically taught at senior-secondary level which are unfeasible for bodies like schools to be made available to the students. Such experiments are available at higher level institutions like AIIMS/IITs/NITs/IIITs.

For example: Linear Air track, CRO machine, Young’s Double Experiment, Ore Refining, Analysis of celestial bodies through telescope, Weather forecasting, Rock age estimation, etc.
