Tech Development and Training

This program includes development of the technological environment in schools. Proper training will be given to students as well as teachers in different fields of technologies. Skiome will ensure a technologically developed and advanced environment for teaching and learning in the school. The process will include continuous workshops.

Some of the fields covered under this domain, includes:

→Coding, Web Development, App development, UI/UX: Introduction to such courses would lead to the development of teams in the school itself which will develop and work on school websites and apps in future. It will also help the students who want to pursue their career in these fields which would give them a head-start for learning languages and programming. Also, we will help the students build their profiles on authenticated competitive coding platforms and environments like hackerrank, codechef, codeforces, vs code, blueJ, intelliJ etc. Also it will expose them to different operating systems like Linux, ubuntu, Windows, MacOs, etc.

→Microsoft Office Full course with all activation keys: This will provide students with a comprehensive understanding of all the elements of Office that are accessible, many of which they are unaware of, but could be very useful to them in day to day life.

→Computer Software + Hardware Knowledge: These would include all the internal machinery processes by the computer when we do simple tasks like web browsing, reading and writing the data, etc. Important keywords like registers, chips, CPU, memory, disks will be introduced to the students to give them an idea about the internal machinery working. This would also open up different paths for students interested in fields like robotics.

→Editing, Manipulating, Designing etc.: Various features such as graphic designing, video/photo editing and after-effects are covered under this course. This opens up doors for fields like graphic designing and animation etc. Many features like Adobe premiere pro, Figma, Frammers, Canva, after effects, etc are provided to students to practise and develop their skills.

→Simulations: Simulated labs created by Skiome will be provided to the students for understanding and experimenting different concepts of different subjects just by sitting at their homes.These simulations initiate the functioning of an existing process which play a major role when it comes to explaining/grasping topics which are practically not possible to demonstrate in the class. Examples include medical surgery, shifting of tropical belts, blood pressure measuring, constellation visualisation, etc.

→Site surfing and cyber security: Under this course,students are taught about the different kinds of cyber threats, attacks, methodologies and the techniques used by the crackers to attack different websites and servers. They are taught about the importance of data management and protection. Also, in site-surfing, they are taught to search the data from different search engines smartly.
