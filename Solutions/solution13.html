Career Counselling

The present norms set by society focus only on some of the major promising jobs, which consist of only 1% of all the total opportunities available in the market. One of the best examples explaining this is the present rat race at the senior secondary level where the majority of the students opt for either the Engineering or the Medical field, the reason being the fact that there is no career counselling within schools to provide proper guidance.

This creates a burden in the minds of students since everyone is not interested in following the crowd, but societal pressure forces them to do so. Skiome brings in the solution to this problem by setting up career counselling centres for the schools associated with it. We provide information about all the different varieties of promising jobs available in the present market and also provide assistance and methods to students to build up their careers in those fields.
