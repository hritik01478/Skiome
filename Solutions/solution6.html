Surveys

We conduct a wide variety of open surveys on different topics. These are conducted by the body and are held in all the schools under Skiome in a given area. The schools are given a particular time interval in which the students have to conduct surveys. The subject report and presentation have to be prepared by the students and then these are presented to our panel members. These surveys will give an exposure to the students to the social environment around them and will also help them to know about the ground-level ideologies of the society. They will learn about data collection and management. These surveys generally include topics from social, financial, historical, environmental,architectural fields, etc.
