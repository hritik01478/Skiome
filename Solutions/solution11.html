AR/VR (Metaverse Room)

We will be introducing the students to the concept of AR/VR where they will be taught about the upcoming futuristic technologies and also experiencing them in virtual surroundings. Skiome is currently working on building Metaverse rooms where students can experience a whole different world around them. Metaverse rooms are created to generate immersive environments for students on subjects like geography, english, history, astronomy, botany and zoology, providing the students with experiences which they would probably encounter in the real world.
