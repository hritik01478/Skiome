Project making and Exhibition

We conduct various projects amongst the schools. These projects will be conducted on a yearly basis.

Skiome will be conducting one inter-exhibition where these projects will be displayed. Skiome will make sure that the good ones are rewarded and get good recognition as well. We also conduct joint exhibitions in an area where all the schools, institutes and colleges under Skiome in that given town/city can participate together. The best projects will be showcased in the exhibitions which would motivate students to create their own such projects and innovations.
