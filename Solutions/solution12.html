Parallel Curriculum and Cross-Platform Learning

Skiome does not intend to or focuses on varying the present school curriculum, rather it helps in assisting the schoolsâ€™ system and making it more efficient and technologically upgraded.

Skiome parallelly runs a system along with the schools which assists the teachers in teaching the concepts better by providing them various innovative services such as objects, models, experiments etc.; it also assists the students in developing their skills and talents by providing them services such as clubs, events, workshops, competitions etc.

The services by Skiome to the schools functions in the same way a stick works in helping a climber to grow, i.e. we are not affecting the basic fundamentals of the curriculum but powering up the education system to a better growth rate.

Skiome is creating an interface to provide a flow of knowledge, talents and skill from one school to another or one city/town to another through frequent interactions between the students and teachers of various schools.

To do this, we are organizing various Open workshops, Educational events, Meetings, and Conferences on various topics, where the teachers and students of different participate, interact and learn skills from each other, providing a better flow of knowledge and network-building within schools.
